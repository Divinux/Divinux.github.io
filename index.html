Welcome!



Self proclaimed indy dev working alone, and therefore wearing many hats. Released a couple of small games, with some of them making it to Steam, if that counts for anything. Mostly doing prototypes however just to explore new ideas.

Currently, I am working fulltime for a quaint family-run business, and loving every minute of it. In my limited free time however, I do not mind helping out on charitable or particularly wholesome projects.
